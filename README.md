# Autonomous Marine Frameworks
A collection of open architectures, technical whitepapers, and defensive disclosures for closed-loop calibration, adaptive sampling, and automated spatial-temporal uncertainty management in marine, aquatic, and hydrographic survey platforms.

---

## ⚖️ Intellectual Property Notice & Defensive Disclosure
The concepts, functional logic, operational protocols, closed-loop triggers, and adaptive sampling workflows disclosed in this repository are **explicitly dedicated to the public domain**. 

This repository serves as a permanent, timestamped public defensive disclosure intended to establish comprehensive prior art under international patent frameworks. Its objective is to prevent the issuance of restrictive utility patents on these systems by any third party, ensuring that the foundational architecture remains free for open innovation and public practice.

## 🗺️ Future Roadmap & Upcoming Disclosures
The following frameworks are actively being developed and will be disclosed directly into this repository:
*   **Automated Bar-Test Integration**: Transitioning the bar-check from a paused station-keeping deployment to a continuous, dynamically adjusted mechanical sequence integrated with real-time winch telemetry.
*   **Multibeam Uncertainty Field Mapping**: Extending 2D spatial confidence decay models into 3D swath geometry to account for outer-beam refraction anomalies in real time.
